About Terpski

The Terrapin Ski and Snowboard Club (a.k.a. Terpski) is the largest student run organization at the University of Maryland, College Park. Founded in 1960, Terpski has provided the students of the University of Maryland with the raddest ski trips known to man. Each year, Terpski embarks on four journeys in search of the softest snow. Terpskiers spend over 80 hours annually traveling coast to coast on a mission to find scenic views, untouched trails, lasting friendships, and the greatest of memories.
